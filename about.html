About

A supportive school community built for steady growth.

Slivana School supports children from kindergarten through high school with a learning environment focused on confidence, character, and clear communication with families.

Slivana School is based in Kurdistan, Soran and supports children across kindergarten, primary, and high school levels. The school aims to provide a calm and encouraging place to learn while helping students grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

The website is designed to give families one clear place for school information. Instead of sending parents to separate channels, it brings together announcements, podcasts, videos, gallery posts, and contact details in a simple structure.

That makes it easier to stay informed, revisit published media, and reach the school quickly when support or follow-up is needed.

Our Mission

To support young learners through thoughtful teaching, personal attention, and a balanced school experience that respects both academic and personal growth.

Our Vision

To help students grow into confident, curious, creative young people with the values and resilience they need for the future.
