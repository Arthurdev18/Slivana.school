About Slivana School

A premier institution dedicated to excellence in education

Slivana School is a premier educational institution in Kurdistan Soran, dedicated to providing high-quality education for kindergarten and primary students. Founded in 2017, we have been committed to fostering a nurturing environment where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower young minds through innovative teaching methods, personalized attention, and a holistic approach to education.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that prepares students for a bright future in a rapidly changing world.

Our Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Respect and Integrity
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Community and Collaboration
