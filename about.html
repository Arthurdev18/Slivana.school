About Slivana School

Learn more about the school’s focus, mission, and the values behind its learning environment.

Slivana School is an educational institution in Kurdistan Soran that serves kindergarten, primary, and high school students. The school is focused on building a learning environment where children can grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

The website brings together core school information, current updates, podcasts, videos, and contact details in one place so families can move through it more easily.

Our Mission

To support young learners through thoughtful teaching, personal attention, and a balanced approach to learning.

Our Vision

To help students build confidence, curiosity, creativity, and strong character for the future.
